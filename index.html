Every day 1.4 billion cups of coffee are consumed worldwide

You must know it's made of coffee beans...

But did you know it can be grown in different ways?

The traditional method is growing coffee plants under a canopy of other trees. Coffee trees can be planted in existing forests or the canopy can be planted around them.

The most popular approach nowadays is growing coffee in full sun. This requires a lot of space and leads to deforestation.

Shade-grown coffee grows slower and produces less, but has a lifespan of up to 30 years, twice the sun-grown tree lifespan.
